puffinmail.io - FAQ

What is puffinmail.io?

puffinmail.io is a temporary mail service. It gives you an email inbox and will allow you to receive email at the address for 30 minutes.

What is wrong with all the other temporary mail services?

They tend to be slow, have terrible UI, have lots of ads and trackers, and transfer 10x the amount of data that puffinmail.io does.

What is puffinmail.io built with?

puffinmail.io is built in pure Go. It doesn't use any JavaScript and uses custom built css.

What kind of server is puffinmail.io running on?

puffinmail.io is currently deployed on kubernetes cluster running in Hetzner, Germany.

Who is Smoking puffin?

Puffin is the mascot of puffinmail.io. He is smoking pipe.

How long do my email messages live in your database?

Emails live only as long as your inbox does, plus a bit more. All email messages have an expiry set to the same time as your inbox. After they expire, they are marked for deletion and are typically deleted within 1 hour.

Do you read my messages or otherwise harvest the content?

No.

Really?

Yep. I don't look at your messages.

How do you make money off this then?

I don't. If by some miracle this takes off and server bills get out of hand, I may look for donations or sponsors. I promise I will never put terrible user-hostile ads on though.

I still don't trust you. Can I run my own puffinmail?

Absolutely. Go check out the instructions on the Github page for how to host your own. Thanks to Hayden Woodhead for idea and original source code.
